    What is GOP size? GOP (Group of Pictures) is a set of video frames for MPEG-4 and H.264 format compression, indicating a collection of frames from the initial I-Frame (key frame) to the next I-Frame. GOP consists of 2 kinds of frames: I-Frame and P-Frame. I-Frame is the basic frame for the compression, also known as Key Frame, which contains one complete image data.
